How to use ThumbCraft

  • Backgrounds: Set a solid color, gradient, or upload an image. If you upload a video, it will extract 5 frames automatically so you can pick the best shot!
  • Text & Badges: Click the text buttons on the left to add text. Click on the text in the canvas to move it around and style it on the right panel.
  • Shapes & Stickers: Add basic shapes or emojis. Select them to change size, color, and rotation.
  • Upload Image (Face): Click the floating "Add Image" button at the bottom right to overlay your own photos (like a cutout of your face) onto the canvas.
  • YT Viewer: Click the "YT Viewer" button in the top navigation to download existing thumbnails from YouTube or grab thumbnails from direct video links.
