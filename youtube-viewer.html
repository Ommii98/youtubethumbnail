YouTube Thumbnail Viewer

How to use this tool:

  • YouTube Videos: Paste any standard YouTube URL (e.g., youtube.com/watch?v=...) to instantly fetch high-quality thumbnails.
  • Direct Videos: Paste a direct video link ending in .mp4, .webm, or .mov to automatically generate a thumbnail frame.
  • Images: Paste a direct image link (.png, .jpg) to quickly grab and copy the source.
